# European Bank Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a European bank using SQL and Power BI. The objective is to identify key factors influencing customer attrition, understand customer demographics, and provide actionable insights to improve customer retention and business performance.

## Problem Statement

Customer churn is a critical challenge for banks as losing customers directly impacts revenue and growth. This project aims to analyze customer data to identify patterns and factors associated with churn, enabling data-driven retention strategies.

## Key Insights

* Certain geographic regions exhibited higher churn rates.
* Inactive members were more likely to churn than active members.
* Customers with lower product engagement showed increased churn risk.
* Age and account balance demonstrated significant influence on customer retention.
* Credit score trends helped identify high-risk customer segments.

## Business Recommendations

* Develop targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
* Improve engagement strategies for inactive members.
* Introduce personalized banking products for different customer segments.
* Strengthen customer relationship management initiatives.
* Monitor churn indicators through continuous dashboard reporting.
